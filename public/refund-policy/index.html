Refund Policy

We aim to describe products clearly before payment so issues are reduced. If there is a genuine problem, we review it fairly and based on the product that was sold.

How refunds are handled

Orders are reviewed and confirmed before payment. If a device has a verified fault within the stated check period or warranty period, we review the issue and may offer an exchange, repair support, store credit, or refund depending on the problem and stock availability.

A refund is not automatic just because a customer changes their mind after purchase. We first compare the complaint against the original listing, condition notes, and the support terms given at the time of sale.

Products must remain in the same condition as sold. Physical damage, water damage, misuse, software tampering, or changes made after purchase are not covered.

Items that usually cannot be refunded

Some items are generally final sale unless they arrive with a verified issue that was not disclosed.

Gift cards, accessories opened after sale, and products sold with clearly stated cosmetic or battery-related condition notes are normally not refundable for those already-disclosed issues.

How to request help

Contact us on WhatsApp or by email as soon as possible with your order details, the product name, and a clear explanation of the issue.

Photos, video, or a short description of the fault help us review the case faster and decide the correct next step.
